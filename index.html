Biography

I am a CS senior at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. Currently, I am working as a Remote Intern at the SHI Labs, University of Oregon with Professor Humphrey Shi. I am also working as a Research Intern with the Picsart AI Research (PAIR) team at Picsart. At IIT Roorkee, I am the Co-President of a Deep Learning-centric discussion group named the Vision and Language Group.

My current research interests broadly revolve around computer vision and deep learning. I have led several projects in the field of image segmentation and inpainting during the last couple of years. I am eager to explore the field of 3D computer vision in the coming months. You can find a list of my publications below.
