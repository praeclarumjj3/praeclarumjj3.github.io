Biography

I am a 1st Year Ph.D. student (advised by Professor Humphrey Shi) in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech. I worked with the same advisor during my undergrad years as a part of SHI Labs. I earned my Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Engineering in 2023 from IIT Roorkee.

My current research interests broadly revolve around developing multimodal vision systems. I have led several projects in image segmentation and inpainting during my undergraduate years. Reach out if you are interested in my research or would like to discuss any ideas.

I spend my free time reading books (Goodreads) or watching web series. I also keep a log of my books. Every once in a while, I write blogs based on my experiences on Medium and pen down some of my thoughts. In the sports column, I like to stay updated about the happenings in the Formula 1 and Cricket worlds.
