Biography

I am a CS junior at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. Currently, I am working as a Remote Intern at the SHI Lab, University of Oregon with Professor Humphrey Shi. I am also working as a Research Intern with the Picsart AI Research (PAIR) team at Picsart.

At IIT Roorkee, I am a member of a Deep Learning-centric discussion group named the Vision and Language Group. I also hold the position of a devloper and project manager at the Mobile Development Group.
